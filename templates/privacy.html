Privacy Policy

QuirkPress is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit QuirkPress site.

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • "Cookies" are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. For more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
  • "Log files" track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use your Personal Information, as described above. For example, we use Google Analytics to help us understand how our customers use the Site.

Data Retention

When you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us via email, we will maintain your information for our records unless and until you ask us to delete this information.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at [admin@quirkpress.com].
